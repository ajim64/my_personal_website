Hi, I'm Hazim. I was born in Negeri Sembilan and raised in Pahang. I really like anything about music, no matter the musical instrument or the song, because for me, music is a way to convey one's feelings. In my free time, I like to listen to songs because music can relax my mind and I like to explore more genres of songs that match my taste. Not only that, playing guitar is also my most interested hobby because I can improve my skills in plucking the guitar for all kinds of songs and also I can create my own songs. Listening or playing music is my joy and my way of relaxing.

My Goals

What I want to achieve in life is to be a useful human being and be dutiful to my parents. I also want to be a musician and create songs that inspire me and the people who listen to them. I hope that one day I can share my work with the public through collaborations or something like that.
